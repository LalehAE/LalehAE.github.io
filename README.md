## About Me
<div style="text-align: justify;">

I am a Lead Data Scientist with 7+ years of experience building machine learning, analytics, and applied AI systems for complex business problems. My work focuses on turning messy, fragmented, and unstructured data into practical tools that support better decisions.

Most recently, at TREK Engineering in Winnipeg, I led AI and data modernization work for geotechnical engineering data. I helped transform legacy scanned reports and scattered historical files into structured, searchable data assets, with validation workflows that allowed domain experts to review and improve data quality.

On top of this data foundation, I built AI tools that allow engineers and researchers to ask questions in natural language, search technical reports, query structured databases, view results on maps, and understand which data and filters were used in each answer. My focus was not only on building AI features, but also on making the systems reliable, explainable, and useful in real workflows.

My best work happens between domain experts, product, and engineering teams: understanding how people work, clarifying the problem, designing the data and AI approach, and delivering solutions that teams can trust and use.

### AI Lab – LLM & AI Systems
<br>

<img align="left" width="170" height="170" src="images/TREK_AI_Assistant.png"> **Geotechnical AI Assistant over Enterprise Data and Reports**
<div style="text-align: justify;">
At TREK Engineering, I designed an AI assistant that helps engineers and researchers ask complex geotechnical questions across structured databases and technical reports. The system uses an agentic LLM workflow to translate multi-step questions into database and geospatial queries, while keeping sensitive company data inside internal systems.

The assistant returns map-based results, downloadable outputs, and explanations of which data, filters, and reasoning were used. It also supports chat-based exploration of PDF reports, helping technical and non-technical users access information more efficiently.

</div>

<img align="left" width="170" height="170" src="images/TREK_Data_Platform.png"> **Geotechnical Data Platform and Expert Feedback Loop**
<div style="text-align: justify;">
I redesigned TREK’s geotechnical data foundation by consolidating thousands of legacy files into structured databases using automated ETL, NLP, and LLM-assisted extraction and cleaning. I also built map-based review workflows that allowed engineers to identify, validate, and correct incomplete or low-confidence records.

This work improved data accessibility, created a stronger foundation for analytics and AI applications, and helped teams reuse historical engineering knowledge more effectively.

### Other Projects


<img align="left" width="170" height="170" src="images/Aircraft.png"> **Aircraft Turnaround**
<div style="text-align: justify;">
I developed a computer vision solution to monitor aircraft turnaround activities in real time and support airport operations. Using a YOLO model, the system analyzed CCTV footage, detected key apron events, and streamed structured outputs to live dashboards on Azure.

This helped operations teams identify delays earlier, improve resource planning, and support more efficient turnaround processes.

<a href="https://LalehAE.github.io/pages/Aircraft.html"><strong>Read more</strong></a>

</div>
<br>

<img align="left" width="170" height="170" src="images/Items.png"> **Procurement Invoice Classification**
<div style="text-align: justify;">
I developed a CatBoost model to classify procurement invoices into more than 30 categories, achieving 86% macro-average accuracy. The model improved direct spend analysis, made hidden costs more visible, and supported better procurement decision-making.

I also optimized the post-processing pipeline, reducing runtime by about 30%.

<a href="https://LalehAE.github.io/pages/Items.html"><strong>Read more</strong></a>

</div>
<br>

<img align="left" width="170" height="170" src="images/Content.png"> **Content Recommender Engine**
<div style="text-align: justify;">
I built a multilingual content recommender using TensorFlow Recommenders, running on Kubeflow and orchestrated with Apache Airflow on GCP. I extended the system to support more than 100 languages and improved filtering logic so users received more current and relevant content.

Planned next steps included A/B testing to measure engagement and refine recommendation quality.
  
<a href="https://LalehAE.github.io/pages/Content.html"><strong>Read more</strong></a>

</div>
<br>

<img align="left" width="170" height="170" src="images/Social.png"> **Social Matching and Recommendation Prototype**
<div style="text-align: justify;">
I created a proof of concept for social matching that used deep learning to connect people with similar interests and goals. The system used LaBSE embeddings and cosine similarity to compare user profiles and improve match relevance.

This project explored how multilingual embeddings can support more meaningful recommendations in community and networking platforms.

<a href="https://LalehAE.github.io/pages/Social.html"><strong>Read more</strong></a>

</div>
<br>

<img align="left" width="170" height="170" src="images/Cohort.png"> **Cohort Churn Analysis**
<div style="text-align: justify;">
I performed cohort-based churn analysis across different membership types to understand retention patterns over time. Working with stakeholders, I helped define churn, identify the required data, and build live churn tables and dashboards.

The insights helped leadership understand membership behavior and supported strategic decisions about retention and membership focus.
